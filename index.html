Rutherford's Gold Foil Experiment

This simple model shows how alpha particles behave when they hit a thin gold foil. Most go straight through, but a few bounce back — proving that atoms have a small, dense nucleus.

Observation

  • Most alpha particles pass straight — atom is mostly empty space.
  • Some bend slightly — they come close to the nucleus.
  • A few bounce back — they hit the dense, positive nucleus.
