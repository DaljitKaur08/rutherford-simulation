Rutherford's Gold Foil Experiment

This simple model shows how alpha particles move when they hit a thin gold foil. Most pass straight through, but some bounce back — showing that atoms have a small, dense nucleus.

What We See

  • Most alpha particles pass straight → atoms are mostly empty space.
  • Some bend → they pass near the nucleus.
  • A few bounce back → they hit the dense positive nucleus.
